Ocean Sustainability Nonprofit Organization

About Us:

Our non-profit organization focuses on ocean sustainability with a goal to preserve, protect, and defend the oceans. We hope to spread awareness on how our oceans affect our every lives such as the air we breathe, food, climate regulation, and transportation.

PROJECTS

Ocean State of Mind: Ocean State of Mind is based off in San Francisco focusing on the reduction of plastic pollution in Ocean Beach. The project encourages beach-goers or volunteers to throw away at least five pieces of trash before leaving the beach and any nearby waterways. As millions of tons of plastic end up in our oceans every year, small efforts will result in huge impacts in protecting our oceans from plastic pollution.

Blue Bio: The organization will focus on monitoring the biodiversity, health and growth of the reef system surrounding Monterey Bay. The volunteers will be gathering raw data through fish, invertebrate, and coral surveys while using scuba equipment. We have been supplying our projects with our donations and fundraisers. It'd mean a lot if you donated to our Nonprofit organization so we can continue our projects.

Ocean Institute: Volunteers are needed to help us spread their marine passion through educating the general public and local outreach. The organization is located in Northern California and volunteers will be facilitating education sessions with students and local communities.
